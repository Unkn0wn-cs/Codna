about

cat mission.txt

What this club actually is, and who it's for.

ICPC Club is the college's competitive programming society. We prepare teams of three for the ACM-ICPC pipeline — local rounds, regionals, and (if we're lucky and sharp) the World Finals. Along the way we also run for Codeforces, AtCoder, and LeetCode practice, because rating goes up either way.

We're not just for people who already know what a segment tree is. Roughly half of our active members joined with under 50 problems solved anywhere. What we care about is reps: showing up, getting stuck, and getting unstuck with people who'll actually explain the trick instead of just linking the editorial.

Every member gets matched with a guided track — a rolling list of topics and hand-picked problems scaled to where they are, reviewed weekly by a coach. That's what the member dashboard below is for.
